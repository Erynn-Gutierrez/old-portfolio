WHO I AM

Hey, my name is Erynn. I'm a rising third-year student at Tufts University studying Cognitive and Brain Science and Computer Science. I'm based between New York and Boston, and am excited to studying abroad in Copenhagen with DIS in the spring of 2024.

A Filipina from New York, I love a hot bowl of Sinigang as much as I love a stacked NYC bagel. I spend most of my time maintaining a solid caffeine buzz and ideating some sort of knitting or crochet project to keep my hands busy with.

Some more fun facts about me: growing up around music, I did some cool performances, from singing on stage with David Byrne to singing on Wye Oak's 2020 album, No Horizon. I'm very passionate about community, immersing myself across several volunteer opportunities across the years, from teaching NYC elementary students attending under-resourced public schools reading, writing, and math with the Go Project, to handpacking meals to NYC residents with serious illnesses with God's Love We Deliver.

I'm currently seeking opportunities to grow my knowledge of user design practices. I'm interested in cognition and perception through a computational perspective. Combined with my strong values in diverse teamwork, I strive to employ my ever-evolving skills from my multitude of experiences in education, volunteerism, and advocacy through all aspects of my work.
